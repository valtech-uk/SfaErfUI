How to choose an apprenticeship

Standards show what apprentices will learn during their apprenticeships.

 

How standards are delivered

 

Training providers teach the skills listed in standards.

 

You don’t have to choose a local provider. Many of them offer distance learning.

 

Sometimes providers will come to you to train your apprentice. At other times your apprentice will travel to them.

 

Find a training provider

 

What can you deliver yourself?

 

You can carry out training yourself, but it could affect your funding.

 

You can also assess your apprentice in-house, but you must:

• do it impartially

• involve a third party

• provide supporting evidence like employee records
