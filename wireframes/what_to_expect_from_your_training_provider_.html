How to employ an apprentice

What to expect from your training provider?

As well as teaching your apprentice, training providers can also support you in other ways.

 

Recruitment

 

Providers may handle all or part of the recruitment process, if you want them to.

 

They often:

 

• advertise vacancies, by writing adverts or sending templates

• check candidates are eligible for apprenticeships

• shortlist candidates for interviews

• contact candidates after interviews

• register apprentices

 

Some providers charge extra for these services.

 

Assessment

 

Your provider will work with you to find an assessment organisation.

 

Payments and funding

 

All apprenticeship funding is routed through your provider, who’ll also:

 

• tell you whether you’re eligible for incentive payments

• pass incentive payments to you

• send information about your apprenticeship to the government
