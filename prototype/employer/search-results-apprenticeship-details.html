What does an actuarial technician do?

Actuarial technicians use statistics to predict how the finances of people and businesses will change. They carry out a number of jobs, from setting car insurance premiums to helping customers plan for retirement.

What skills do actuarial technicians need?

Actuarial technicians need to be comfortable:

  • working with numbers
  • meeting tight deadlines
  • earning to use software quickly
  • thinking logically
  • dealing with clients
  • paying attention to details
