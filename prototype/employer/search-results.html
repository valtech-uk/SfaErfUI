Carpenters in Carpenters and joiners

  • Apprenticeship standard for advanced carpentry and joinery

    Actuarial technicians use statistics to predict how the finances of people and businesses will change. They carry out a number of jobs, from setting car insurance premiums to helping customers plan for retirement.

    Learn more

    • How long does the apprenticeship last?

      The apprenticeship usually takes 2-3 years.

    • What qualifications does the apprenticeship lead to?

      This is a level 4 apprenticeship.

      Apprentices who complete it also become members of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries.
